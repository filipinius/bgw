04 / FAQ

Driver questions

What do I need to drive for BGW Trucking?

A valid, unrestricted Class A CDL, an acceptable motor vehicle record, and a current DOT medical card. Verifiable OTR experience is preferred, but recent CDL-A graduates are encouraged to apply.

Do you hire owner-operators and company drivers?

Yes. We bring on both owner-operators running under our authority and company drivers in our equipment.

Where is BGW Trucking based?

We're based in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania (17050), running regional and long-haul interstate lanes out of Central PA.

How soon can I start after applying?

After you submit the short application, ownership reaches out directly — no call center — then orientation and dispatch follow quickly.

Is BGW Trucking a safe carrier?

Yes. We have zero reportable crashes and zero out-of-service events over the last 24 months, so driving with us protects your CSA.

Ready to roll with us?

The application takes two minutes. You can even attach your CDL right from your phone.
